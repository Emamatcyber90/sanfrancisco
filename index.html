Learn How to Program

Learn how to program in Node.js with us, from the basic level. If you already know some JavaScript, will be a big difference, otherwise, do not worry, we will help you.

Self Guided

Nodeschool modules are self-guided. No boring lectures. All challenges are open source and are available online at nodeschool.io. This means that you can continue learning after the event.

For All

We think that learning to program is great and everyone should feel welcome at our event. This includes our expectation that all participants be kind to each other and respect our Code of Conduct

.
