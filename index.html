Learn How to Program

Nodeschool is for people completely new to programming, as well as experienced programmers looking to try a new language, or front-end developers learning server-side programming.

Knowing some JavaScript will help, but is not necessary to attend a Nodeschool event.

Self-Directed

Nodeschool lessons are self-guided. No boring lectures. All challenges are open source and are available online at nodeschool.io. This means that you can continue learning after the event.

For All

We think that learning to program is great and everyone should feel welcome at our event. This includes our expectation that all participants be kind to each other and respect our Code of Conduct
